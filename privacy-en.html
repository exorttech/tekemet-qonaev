Privacy Policy

This document describes how the site processes contact data and how a user can contact the hotel and restaurant.

1. What data may be transmitted

The site does not collect user accounts and does not use registration forms. When a user follows the WhatsApp link or sends an email, they themselves transmit contact data and the message text to the chosen service.

2. Purposes of processing

Collected data is used only to contact the guest, process room bookings, clarify menu information, or respond to requests the user has voluntarily submitted.

3. Channels of communication

Communication is carried out via WhatsApp and email. These third‑party services process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Cookies and local storage

The site may use the browser's local storage to remember theme choice and admin state. No external analytics or advertising pixels are present in the code.

5. Contacts

For questions about data processing, please contact TEKEMET RESTO-HOTEL by email at tekemet2025@mail.ru or by phone at +7 707 202 5888.
